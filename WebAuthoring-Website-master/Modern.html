Internet now

The internet nowadays is mainly used for social media purposes and connecting people all around the world with different software apps such as Whatsapp, Discord, Twitter, Facebook and Instagram.

These services are used to let people connect with other people online around the world to share stories, art, ideas and much more. These apps like twitter get around 206 million daily active users and thoses users make over 500 million Tweets per day so these types of sites are very popular and have a very big impact on thousands of people every day.

Sites like Youtube, Twitch and Tictok are also very popular websites that are used for people to upload video content that are used to entertain people and even provide people with jobs. There are plenty of people on these sites that gain jobs by just creating content for people online, on youtube alone 3.7m new videos are uploaded to YouTube every day, and generates around 28.8 billion dollars a year. This site alone proves that there is a massive market out there for user created content that people like to watch and support.

1969

It's date when in Internet was "Borning". In 1969 Advanced Research Project Agency run first in the world wide-area packet-switched network with distributed control and one of the first networks to implement the TCP/IP protocol suite. This project was created to share science research documents and results between laboratories in different sides of country and keep safe all researches in different storages what was splited around all country.
